Built-In Quality

We start by manufacturing top quality pallets. We use only high-quality #2 grade Douglas Fir and Southern Yellow Pine lumber that is responsibly forested in North America. Pallets are kiln-dried to prevent infestation, mold, and moisture. No chemical treatments or hazardous materials are ever used on PECO pallets.

Block Pallets: The Better Choice

PECO’s block pallets are much stronger and more durable than conventional stringer pallets. Our standard GMA (48”x40”) pallets are designed to hold up to 2,800-pound loads (edge-rackable)
